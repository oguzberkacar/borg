<h1 align="center">borg</h1>
<p align="center"><b>Ultra-light macOS menu-bar organizer.</b><br>
Hide the icons you don't need. Reveal them on demand.</p>

---

## Download

**[⬇︎ Download borg (latest)](https://github.com/oguzberkacar/borg/releases/latest)**

Notarized by Apple — no "unidentified developer" warning. Requires macOS 13+.

1. Download the `.zip`, unzip, drag **borg** to `/Applications`.
2. Launch it — a `‹` icon appears in your menu bar with a quick guide.
3. Hold **⌘** and drag your menu-bar icons across the `◇` / `◆` separators to
   choose what hides. Click `‹` (or press ⌃⌥B) to toggle.

borg auto-updates itself via [Sparkle](https://sparkle-project.org).

## Features

- Click / global-hotkey toggle
- Auto-hide after inactivity · hover-to-expand
- Always-hidden section for icons you never want to see
- Collapse when an app goes fullscreen
- Behavior profiles · launch at login
