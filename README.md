## Setup — 30 seconds

1. Open the `.dmg`, drag **borg** into **Applications**, launch it.
2. First launch shows a short guide. A `‹` appears in your menu bar.
3. Hold **⌘** and drag your menu-bar icons across borg's separators:
   - Left of **◇** → hides when you collapse (click `‹` or press **⌃⌥B**).
   - Left of **◆** → always hidden (optional zone).

> First launch on macOS 15+ may need **System Settings → Privacy & Security → Open Anyway** once. borg is Apple-notarized; this is the standard gatekeeper step for apps outside the App Store.

## Features

- **Toggle** by click or global hotkey (⌃⌥B, rebindable)
- **Auto-hide** after inactivity · **hover-to-expand**
- **Always-hidden** section for icons you never want to see
- **Auto-show new icons** — newly added menu-bar icons jump to the visible side (experimental, needs Accessibility)
- **Collapse on fullscreen** · behavior **profiles** · **launch at login**
- **Self-updating** via [Sparkle](https://sparkle-project.org)
